## What It Does

### Conversational Command Center
One thread. No menus, no tabs, no dashboard. You type or speak what you need, and LocalGenius does it. "Post something about our new menu" becomes a social post, written, scheduled, and published — with one tap to approve.

### Website Generation
In your first five minutes, LocalGenius builds a professional, mobile-optimized website from your business name, a few photos, and a short conversation. Your hours, your reviews, your booking button — live and published before you finish your coffee.

### Review Management
New Google review at 10pm? LocalGenius drafts a response that sounds like you, not a corporation. Positive reviews get handled automatically if you trust it. Negative reviews always get flagged for your personal touch. You approve with one tap.

### Social Posting
"Post something about our Tuesday special." That's all it takes. LocalGenius writes the copy, picks your best photo, and schedules it for when your customers are looking. Instagram, Facebook, Google — all from one sentence.

### Email & SMS Campaigns
"Send a reminder to customers who haven't visited in 30 days." Done. No templates to build, no lists to import, no campaign workflows to configure. You describe what you want in plain language, and it happens.

### Local SEO
LocalGenius continuously keeps your Google Business Profile current — description, keywords, hours, holiday schedules. You don't manage it. You don't think about it. Your listing stays sharp because someone is always watching it.

### Weekly Digest
Every Monday at 7am, you get a 90-second read. Three parts:
- **Here's what happened.** Your numbers, in plain language, with context. "340 website visits, up 12% from last week."
- **Here's what I did.** Every action LocalGenius took, listed plainly. "Posted 3 times on Instagram. Responded to 4 reviews."
- **Here's what I recommend.** One actionable suggestion with a single tap to approve.

No dashboards. No analytics screens. Just a clear picture from someone you trust.

## How It Works

**Five minutes.** That's the onboarding.

1. Tell us your business name, what you do, and where you are.
2. We search Google, Yelp, and social media for your existing presence.
3. Upload a few photos of your space, your team, your best work.
4. Tell us what matters most: getting found, managing reviews, or staying active on social.
5. We show you a professional website, a social post ready to publish, an optimized Google listing, and a suggested first campaign. All built from what you gave us.

One tap to publish everything. Then the conversation begins.

You talk. We handle the rest.
